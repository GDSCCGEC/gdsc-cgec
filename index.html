    ABOUT GDSC CGEC

    Google Developers Student Clubs (GDSC) is a community build for like-minded college and university students with an interest in Google developer technologies. The global initiative is aimed at providing a platform for undergraduate and graduate students to connect, learn and grow through various events and activities. Building and forming valuable connections in the process.
